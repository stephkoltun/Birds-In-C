Birds in C

Conduct an orchestra of birds through a re-interpretion of Terry Riley’s “In C”.

Musical phrases have been composed using recorded bird sounds to create an unexpected cacophony of chirps, whistles, and hoots. As the conductor, you can add different birds to the orchestra and advance each of them sequentially from one phrase to the next.

Click the shapes to hear the different sounds you'll be playing.

They represent Terry Riley's original 53 short melodic fragments. His instructions dictated that the phrases be played sequentially, but each musician could determine how many times they repeat each phrase before moving to the next.
